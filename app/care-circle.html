Treatment plan is set

Just had the big planning appointment. 7 weeks of concurrent radiation and chemotherapy starting April 1. It'll be Monday through Friday for radiation, with cisplatin infusions weekly.

Got my port placed yesterday — that wasn't fun but it's done. Also got the custom radiation mask fitted. Feels like something out of a sci-fi movie.

I'm nervous but honestly relieved to have a plan. The waiting and not knowing was the hardest part. Let's do this.

Hey everyone. Rather than trying to keep up with a hundred texts and calls (which I love, but it's a lot right now), I'm going to post updates here so you can all follow along.

I'm doing okay. It's a lot to process but I've got an incredible support system. Thank you all for being part of it.
